About


Mahyoobah Eco Solutions was established as a private limited company in 2009 under the proprietorship of Mr. Kunjabdulla. He actively advocated the importance of a holistic, complete waste management solution for society, which ultimately led him to begin this enterprise. Our company collects all kinds of solid waste and sends it to the right destinations for recycling. Thereby, ensure the seamless utilization of collected waste. With over 10 years of experience in effective waste management, we also serve as a leading consultant to various manufacturing units and companies providing advisory and research services on proper solid waste management. We are also helping several national and multinational brands to manage their Extended Producer Responsibility in India.Mahyoobah Eco Solutions Pvt Ltd is registered as a Startup company under the Kerala Startup Missionand we have a mobile app named “BETO” to coordinate the work of our team members.

Our App

The Beto Mobile app, a remarkable collaboration between Kanhangad Municipality and Mahyoobah Eco Solutions Pvt Ltd, stands as an innovative solution geared towards the effective treatment of inorganic waste. In a span of merely 10 months, the app has proven its prowess by successfully facilitating the collection of a 750 tonnes of inorganic waste. Presently, the app's utilization is limited to the diligent Haritha Karma Sena workers and the commendable employees responsible for the garbage collection vehicle. However, there is a promising prospect on the horizon as the app's services are poised to be extended to the wider public in the near future, fostering a collective effort towards sustainable waste management.
